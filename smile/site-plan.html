Overview

Purpose

Have you ever imaged a world where you have no one to turn to? Imagine our children wondering in the desert of love? Feeble knees reaching the ground for a glimpse of hope. You do not have to wonder anymore with this new, Touch-Help website. The site has pages that can locate lost loved ones and bond us with those in situations very similar to ours. With one touch there are videos of hope and encouragement. This site has been designed to bring smile to your face on any smart device that you can access. It will have an open discussion platform for those who would love a public opinion.

Audience

This site will be targeting many who believe that life is hard only for them, those who have given up and think suicide is the only way out, the many who have been separated from their loved ones and are seeking a platform to find them. In a nutshell, this website is for the young, middle-aged and old to find peace through a world of turmoil. It is designed to reach over 2,000 people each day from all over the world. It will be written in English and translated in atleast 10 more different languages for many non-English speakers to have access.

Content

Home page

Many have experienced loses ranging from the loss of a loved one to the fear of losing themselves. Miraculous Tale shares with you success stories of the many who have made it through these tough times. As they share there experiences, there will be a provition for their contacts, that will enable those users who wish to connect with them, have a chance of connecting with them. This page will also have daily inspiration quotes, specifically for the daily users to be strengthened. Many of these quotes will be saved and can easily be accesed. The opening photo will portray how we feel the society out there is, before they get to discover Miraculous tale. The page will have our contacts, other sites where we can be accessed and links to other pages of our website.

About us

Who we are: Miraculous Tale is a non-profit organization based in Masaka District in Uganda. The organization was formed in the year 2005, following the many cries in Masaka, a district in Uganda. At that time, many people children, wives and husbands were getting lost on a daily basis in many families around the district. As concerned citizens, we reached out to the authorities and managed to have them accept our bid to provide resources in finding the lost individuals. We set up centers for counselling and providing hope for those who did not have any. We managed to ensure a safe environment for the people after so many months of fighting. With so much success in that area, we thought we can continue to bring hope to many by setting up centers in Many different places. It has been a meaningful life experience for us and many who have been helped. We could not have done any better task than being someone's miracle. Our team: Margret Kikanyi: She is the Co-founder of the Organization. She formally worked as a social worker at Hope Fund, an organization that dealt with orphans. She is enthusiastic about bringing peace to the world and has hope that one day the world will be a better place. Esbert Mulony: He is a co-founder and currently the executive director of the organization. He has worked with many human rights organizations and saw it fit to create one that reaches to individuals. He has been awarded many times for his relentless effort to bring a smile to people's faces Kith Betort: He works as the lawyer for the organization and also for many law-related cases of those who need help. He has been part of the organization since 2011. He is passionate about his work and serves everyone regardless of their financial status.

FAQ

Is help only available online? No. We reach out to individuals if we see it fit. We help individuals connect with the authorities in many unavoidable instances where loved ones are missing. We also provide one on one counselling sessions for those who have no will to live or have lost hope. For any details about individual reach-out, please call our toll-free number at the bottom of the page and follow the instructions. Can I access information without internet? Yes. You can sign up for updates via text messages. The frequency upon which these messages are sent is personal, you can choose a daily basis, once a week, once in 2 weeks or monthly. Text charges may be incurred. Those who have wifi can also download videos and watch at a future date. Who can be helped? Everyone who is willing to seek for help is welcome. We accommodate the young, old, sick, addicts, as long as you are searching for the lost or feel lonely, you are welcome. Can I volunteer? We believe in diversity of knowledge and are happy to have people from all walks of life help in this cause. Please signup early enough for your intended report day, for better preparation on our side. Where can I find more information about this organization? You can click on ABOUT US, at our home page. It has details of who we are, when we started, why we are in operation, our team members and the location of our offices. Where are you located? We have 30 branches in over 16 African countries, as you click on the ABOUT US icon you will be directed to a location in our country of operation. Who can access the Blog? It is for everyone to share their concerns and have the public give an opinion or a solution over it. The Replies will also be filtered so as to have only those which uplift and have clean language. My situation is private, I don't want to post in public? There are two options before you post, you can choose public or private. For private choices, our services are available 24/7 and someone will always be ready to render help.
