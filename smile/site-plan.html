Overview

Purpose

Have you ever imaged a world where you have no one to turn to? Imagine our children wondering in the desert of love? Feeble knees reaching the ground for a glimpse of hope. You do not have to wonder anymore with this new, Touch-Help website. The site has pages that can locate lost loved ones and bond us with those in situations very similar to ours. With one touch there are videos of hope and encouragement. This site has been designed to bring smile to your face on any smart device that you can access. It will have an open discussion platform for those who would love a public opinion.

Audience

This site will be targeting many who believe that life is hard only for them, those who have given up and think suicide is the only way out, the many who have been separated from their loved ones and are seeking a platform to find them. In a nutshell, this website is for the young, middle-aged and old to find peace through a world of turmoil. It is designed to reach over 2,000 people each day from all over the world. It will be written in English and translated in atleast 10 more different languages for many non-English speakers to have access.
